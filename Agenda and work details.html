Agenda & Efforts

Omus is an organization that provides education to disabled and differently-abled people to make themselves independent. It also provides inspiration and opportunity to participate in works of national interest. Social interest work To ensure participation of disabled people in voter awareness campaign. To ensure participation of disabled people in Swachh Bharat Abhiyan. To ensure participation of disabled people in open defecation campaign. To organize awareness program on hygiene and health of women and disabled persons with the team of Health Department. To organize awareness programs for eradication of epidemics. Distributing free food / food items to the disabled and needy. To distribute clothes, daily use items etc., free of cost to the disabled and needy. Participation of disabled people in School Chalo Abhiyan. Participating children with disabilities in the plantation program. Participation of differently abled people in propagating 1090, and Dial 100 of police administration. Counseling juvenile and juvenile inmates. To provide training and other support in training programs of the Rehabilitation Council of India, New Delhi. Organizing seminars, interviews, etc. for a detailed consideration with experts working for the benefit of persons with disabilities and participating in programs organized in and outside the country for the purpose. To prepare and train training programs for the disabled and specialists working in the field of special education. To motivate disabled people towards Yoga education and to participate in the promotion and propagation of Yoga. Strive for the empowerment of women, especially women with disabilities and to ensure their significant participation in society. To conduct a survey for the identification of children with disabilities. Counseling for the disabled and their parents.

Organizing teacher training program

Organizing teacher training program- To provide training related to teaching training of disability to teachers and other staff from pre primary to higher secondary and senior secondary level. Enrollment of children with disabilities in schools. Helping to improve the talent hidden in the disabled. To provide handicapped children the opportunity and platform exhibit their talent. Assistance in making handicapped certificates of persons with disabilities. Assistance in making UDID cards of persons with disabilities. Assistance in making rail travel and roadways travel passes for disabled people. Providing assistance to persons with disabilities by various government and non-governmental organizations. To organize individual and community rehabilitation programs for persons with disabilities. To arrange educational training of disabled children. Preparation of training training material for persons with disabilities. Exhibition of handicapped people by making handmade material. Disability awareness stalls in programs of various government, non-governmental organizations.
