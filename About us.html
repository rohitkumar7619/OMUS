About OMUS

OMUS is a registered organization since the year 2008 working sincerely in the interest of, which is constantly engaged in building a healthy and strong society within the constitutional scope of the country. For this, OMUS makes a concerted effort to empower disabled and disabled people, women, and disadvantaged sections and bring them into the mainstream of society. At the same time, by encouraging the development of morality in the youth power of the society, it motivates them to take responsibility for the work of social interest.

Our Vision

OMUS is an institution committed to building a healthy and strong society, whose aim is to bring the disabled, women and disadvantaged people into the mainstream of the society as well as empower them and ensure their significant contribution and responsibilities in the establishment of an ideal society.

Our Mission

OMUS strives to reach genuinely needy people to achieve their goals. OMUS tries to reach the slums of poor, backward, etc. people located far away for this work. The most important thing is that OMUS tries to motivate the disabled, women and backward people to work continuously for the benefit of the society.
